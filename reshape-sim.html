Step 4 — rules: rows × cols must equal n
# constraint: rows × cols must equal total elements (n) arr.reshape(4, 4) → ✓ (4×4 = 16) arr.reshape(3, 5) → ✗ ValueError! (3×5 = 15 ≠ 16) arr.reshape(-1, 4) → ✓ (-1 auto-computes = 4 rows)
reshape(r, c) ✓ when r × c = n
4×4 = 8×2 = 16 — both are valid shapes.

3×5 = 15 ≠ 16. Product must exactly equal total elements.
